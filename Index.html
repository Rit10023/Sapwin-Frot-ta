SAPWIN-FROT TAB

Composition: Aceclofenac 100 mg + Paracetamol 325 mg + Serratiopeptidase 15 mg Tablets

Aceclofenac (100mg)

  • NSAID: Reduces pain, inflammation, and fever.
  • Pain relief: Effective for osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and ankylosing spondylitis.

Paracetamol (325mg)

  • Analgesic and antipyretic: Relieves pain and reduces fever.
  • Synergistic effect: Enhances the pain relief when combined with Aceclofenac.

Serratiopeptidase (15mg)

  • Anti-inflammatory enzyme: Breaks down proteins that cause inflammation.
  • Mucolytic effect: Reduces swelling and promotes healing.

Combination Benefits

  • Provides relief from pain, inflammation, and fever.
  • Aceclofenac and Serratiopeptidase together reduce inflammation effectively.
  • Improves mobility by reducing pain and swelling.

Potential Side Effects

  • Gastrointestinal issues: Nausea, vomiting, stomach pain.
  • Allergic reactions: Rash, itching, swelling.
  • Other: Headache, dizziness.

Precautions

  • Consult a healthcare professional before use.
  • Follow dosage instructions to minimize side effects.
